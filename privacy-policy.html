PRIVACY POLICY

BalancedAudio does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

What We Collect

Nothing. BalancedAudio collects zero personal information. No browsing history, no usage data, no identifiers, no analytics.

Local Storage Only

The only data stored is your preferences (ON/OFF state, selected mode, slider values). This data is saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. It never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

No Third Parties

BalancedAudio does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking tools of any kind.

No Network Requests

BalancedAudio makes zero network requests. It operates entirely within your browser using the Web Audio API to process audio locally.

Permissions Explained

storage — Used only to save your mode and slider preferences locally on your device.

tabs — Used only to send your settings to the active tab when you change them in the popup. No tab data is read or stored.

host_permissions (all URLs) — Required to detect and process video elements on any website you visit. No page content is read or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data at all, there are no privacy concerns regarding users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions? Reach out via the Chrome Web Store support page for BalancedAudio.
